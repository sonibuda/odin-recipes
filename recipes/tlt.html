Vegan BLT Sandwich

Description

This is a plant-based version of the classic BLT, easy, quick, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • bread of your choice
  • Veganaise
  • Lightlife Fakin' Bacon smoky tempeh strips
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • sun dried tomatoes, optional, but tasty
  • black pepper
  • a couple of teaspoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Saute the Fakin' Bacon strips in some olive oil in a large skillet.
  2. While the Fakin' Bacon cooks, toast your bread.
  3. If using sun dried tomatoes, chop them into smaller bits.
  4. Spread Veganaise on your toasted bread slices.
  5. Grind fresh black pepper onto your bread slices.
  6. Layer Fakin' Bacon on your prepared bread.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped sun dried tomatoes.
  8. Top with fresh tomato slices.
  9. Add a generous amount of lettuce to help balance out the saltiness of the sandwich.
  10. Enjoy.
