Chana Masala

Description

Chana Masala is a vegan dish created with chickpeas and a spicy tomato-based sauce.

Its originates from Northern Indian Cuisine.

This version of the dish is created by Isa Chandra-Moskowitz, a well-known vegan chef,

and has consistently been the tasiest recipe of Chana Masala on the web.

Ingredients

Spice Blend

  • 1 T plus 1 t. cumin
  • 1 T ground coriander
  • 1 t. turmeric
  • 1/2 t. fennel seeds
  • 1/2 t. cardamom
  • 1/4 t. cinnamon
  • 1/8 t. ground cloves
  • 1/4 t. ground cayenne

All Other Ingredients

  • 3 T coconut oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 jalapenos, deseeded and chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 heaping T fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/4 c. chopped cilantro
  • 3 lbs. tomatoes, diced
  • black pepper
  • 1 t. salt
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 t. agave
  • juice of 1 lime

Steps

  1. Mix together spice blend
  2. Saute onion in coconut oil for about 10 minutes, until browned
  3. Add jalapeno, garlic, and ginger and sautee until fragrant, about 30 seconds
  4. Add the spice blend and toast about 1 minute
  5. Add the tomatoes, salt, pepper, chickpeas, and agave
  6. Cover the pot and cook, covered, about 10 minutes until the tomatoes get saucy
  7. Remove the lid and cook for 20 more min on low heat
  8. Add lime juice
