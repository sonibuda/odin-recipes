Frito Pie

Description

Frito Pie with red chile is a tasty New Mexican classic.

It's created from corn chips, red chile sauce, cheese, beans and garnished with lettuce, tomato, and onion.

Ingredients

  • corn chips, like Fritos
  • frozen red chile, such as Bueno brand. Choose "hot", "medium" or "mild" depending on your personal preference.
  • 2 cans pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • cheddar cheese, shredded
  • tomatoes, diced
  • several green onions, finely chopped
  • lettuce, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Thaw the red chile slightly in the microwave, then transfer to a small saucepan
  2. Heat the chile on medium-low heat and simmer for about 5 minutes
  3. In the meantime, chop the lettuce, tomatoes and onions.
  4. Shred the cheese.
  5. Drain and rinse the cans of beans.
  6. Assemble: place chips in a bowl, top with beans and cheese, pour red chile over everything, then garnish with lettuce, tomato, and onion.
